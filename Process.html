Working process


  1. Briefing

    2. Description of the tasks that the site should solve. Collection of input data, discussion of tasks.

  2. Competitive analysis

    3. Analytical work and market research of a specific industry, product, business.

  3. Prototyping

    4. Designing the logic of the future site. Creation of a sitemap and its structure.

  4. Visual analysis

    5. Selection of references. Monitoring of the latest trends and the formation of the most successful style.

  5. Technical specifications

    6. Preparation of technical documentation for design. A detailed description of the tasks that the site should solve.

  6. Concept

    7. Development of the layout of the future site. Formation of adaptability to popular devices. Graphic, logical prototype.

  7. Development

    8. The transition from a ready-made layout to development. Connecting analytics, data collection and all systems for the functioning of the site.
